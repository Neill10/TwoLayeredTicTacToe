Tic tac toe

This is TWO layered Tic Tac Toe, a spin off of Tic Tac toe.
  • The rules to win are the same as the original but with a twist
  • You may replace a Mark with your own
    (provided that you haven't marked that block the previous turn)
  • To prevent a mark from being taken, you can lock it by placing your mark twice
  • Select a Mark to play as (X or O) and have fun!
